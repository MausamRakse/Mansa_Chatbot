Mansa Infotech

Company Overview:
Mansa Infotech is a pioneering software development company with specific expertise in IT solutions. Founded in 2008, the company has a strong presence in India (Chandigarh) and Florida, USA. It serves clients across Canada, the UK, and the USA. Mansa Infotech transforms ideas into user-friendly products and empowers businesses to thrive in the digital landscape. We foster a culture of innovation, continuous improvement, and work-life balance.
